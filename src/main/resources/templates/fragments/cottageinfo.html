Welcome to Lakeside Paradise, a stunning waterfront cottage nestled in the heart of Muskoka. This beautifully appointed retreat offers breathtaking views of the lake from almost every room.

Enjoy your morning coffee on the spacious deck while watching the sunrise over the water, or spend your evenings gathered around the fire pit making memories with loved ones.

The cottage features an open-concept living area with floor-to-ceiling windows, a fully equipped modern kitchen, three comfortable bedrooms, and two updated bathrooms. The property includes 150 feet of private shoreline, a dock for swimming and boating, and a canoe and kayaks for guest use.
